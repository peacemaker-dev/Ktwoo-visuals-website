Terms & Conditions

1. Booking Confirmation

A booking is only confirmed after a 50% deposit of the total package price has been received by Ktwo Visuals. No deposit means no confirmed booking. Your shoot date will not be reserved until payment has been received and acknowledged.

2. Payment Terms

The remaining 50% balance is due before the final shoot date or in installments as agreed upon in writing by both parties prior to the shoot. For Music Video, Promo Video, and Freelancing services, full payment is required before the shoot or gig date. All prices are starting-from prices unless otherwise confirmed in writing.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the confirmed shoot date may result in cancellation fees at the discretion of Ktwo Visuals. Deposits are non-refundable once the shoot date has been confirmed. Rescheduling is subject to availability and must be communicated as soon as possible.

4. Transportation & Accommodation

Transportation, accommodation, food, and studio booking costs are excluded from all package prices and will be charged separately depending on location. These additional costs will be communicated and agreed upon before the booking is confirmed.

5. Content Delivery

Final edited content will be delivered via a private digital download link once editing is complete and full payment has been received. Ktwo Visuals is not responsible for content after it has been delivered to the client.

6. Revisions

Additional revisions after final delivery are available at an additional cost starting from R300 per revision. Raw footage delivery is available from R1 000 and must be requested before the shoot date.

7. Intellectual Property

Full ownership of the final edited content transfers to the client upon receipt of full payment. Prior to full payment, all content remains the intellectual property of Ktwo Visuals. Ktwo Visuals reserves the right to use final content for portfolio, social media, and promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed upon in writing by both parties.

8. Client Responsibilities

The client is responsible for ensuring the shoot location is accessible, that all necessary permissions are obtained, and that all relevant parties are present and prepared at the agreed time. Ktwo Visuals is not liable for delays caused by the client or third parties.

9. Equipment

Any damage to Ktwo Visuals equipment caused by client negligence will be the financial responsibility of the client. The client will be invoiced for repair or replacement costs where applicable.

10. Limitation of Liability

Ktwo Visuals will make every effort to deliver the agreed services. In the unlikely event of equipment failure, illness, or circumstances beyond our control, Ktwo Visuals will communicate immediately and work to find a suitable resolution. Liability is limited to the deposit amount paid.
