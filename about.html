The Founder

Meet The Man
Behind The Lens

The foundations of KTwo Visuals were laid long before the company officially existed. In its earliest stages, the focus was on experimenting with technology, creating music, and producing entertaining short-form videos that quickly gained attention within local communities. These creative projects helped develop a deeper understanding of visual storytelling, audience engagement, and content production.

In 2019, a breakthrough moment came when a locally produced video titled Inkabi gained significant recognition. The success of this project inspired further collaboration and led to the formation of a creative collective known as the Brotherhood — producing comedy skits and digital content that built a growing audience and earned recognition from established figures within the South African entertainment industry.

As the creative journey progressed, visual storytelling became the primary focus. In 2021, KTwo Visuals took its first major step into professional photography and videography with the acquisition of its first camera — a Canon 550D. What started as street photography quickly expanded into music videos, event coverage, and commercial visual content.
