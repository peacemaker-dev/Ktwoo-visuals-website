  • Full production team
  • Drone coverage included
  • Studio-grade lighting setup

Standard

Balanced Professional Coverage

The most popular choice. A professional team with drone coverage, quality lighting, and solid deliverables at a more accessible price point. Perfect for clients who want professional quality without the full premium production scale.

  • Cinematic reel

Essential Coverage

Please Note

Important Information

Before making a booking, please take note of the following important details that apply across all Ktwo Visuals services. These conditions exist to ensure a transparent, professional, and smooth experience for every client.

Transportation Excluded

Transportation costs are not included in any package price and will be charged separately based on the shoot location and distance.

Accommodation Excluded

For shoots requiring overnight stays or long-distance travel, accommodation costs are the responsibility of the client and are charged separately.

Studio Fees Excluded

If a studio space is required for your shoot, studio hire costs are excluded from all package prices and will be quoted and charged separately.

Booking Confirmed After Deposit

Your shoot date is only secured after a 50% deposit has been received and confirmed. No deposit means no confirmed booking.

Pricing May Vary By Location

All prices are starting-from prices. Final pricing may vary depending on location, travel distance, and any additional production requirements.
