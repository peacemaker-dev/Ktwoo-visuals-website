About This Service

Every Ceremony,
Preserved In Full

Weddings and traditional ceremonies are among the most meaningful events in a person's life — and they deserve to be documented with the same respect, care, and cinematic quality that defines every Ktwo Visuals production. Our freelancing service provides professional videography coverage for weddings, traditional ceremonies, and events of all kinds across South Africa.

We operate on a flat rate structure based on the event type and coverage duration. Equipment is not included in the rate and is handled separately. Transportation, accommodation, and food costs are excluded and will be communicated before your booking is confirmed. Full payment is required before the gig date.
